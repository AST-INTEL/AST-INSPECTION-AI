## Management Gate

<Aside type="caution" title="Decision point — between P.07 and P.08">
At the completion of Phase 7, a working, documented, security-verified system exists. This is the point at which a formal presentation is made to management. The Production track begins only after this approval.

**What management reviews at this gate:**
- Live demonstration of the system on real CANAR inspection data
- Security verification report with adversarial test results
- Performance benchmarks — processing time, report quality comparison
- Production deployment plan and IT requirements
- What is being asked for (see [POC vs Production](/overview/poc-vs-production))
</Aside>

## Duration Notes

Phase durations are honest estimates based on one person working part-time (evenings and weekends) on the POC alongside primary job responsibilities. Each phase has a **minimum** duration set by the complexity of the work, not by available time.

Phases are not started before their predecessors are fully complete and documented. No phase is considered "done" until all deliverables on its checklist are checked off with evidence.
